ProConnect

Responsive web application that connects growing professionals with mentors that can give quality advice one on one.

My Process

I utilized the User Centered Design process and The Design Thinking process for this project.

Project Overview

Objective

Enable anyone, anywhere to instantly chat with a professional in virtually any field.

Problem Statement

Users seeking expert advice need a way to connect with professionals in an efficient and timely manner because they need the support to develop solutions to their daily problems.

We will know this to be true when we see a growing use of the app and feedback from users that the advice which was given by the experts helped them solve their problems.

Risk/Opportunity

The risk for ProConnect is that there are many apps out there to connect with experts already. This one might get lost in the list of those available and make it hard to stand out.

Opportunity lies in combining features to create an app that more specifically meets user's needs. This could ultimately lead to the user being repeat customers and an increase in downloads.

Research

Competitive Analysis

Intro - This is a high rated app that utilizes celebrity and high-profile professionals. They market a video call and communication with the professional for their advice. Because of the high-profile experts, the calls are priced higher than other competitors.

Actio - This app offers free help and live classes. They offer users to have their own class which expands the topics available. Experts only needed an account to have a class so there is no major screening.

User Survey

Most participants stated that they do regularly seek help or advice online, but most go for quick answers from places like google. About half of the participants would consider being a mentor/professional for the app but only on a specific topic.

User Interviews

Many potential users turn to Google first when needing an answer. The challenge for the Expert app is going to be how to get users to use the app instead of just Google.

A lot of users want a quick answer from experts. Adding a feature for experts to post frequent questions may result in users purchasing 1-on-1 sessions to get more details.

It is not a common practice for users to reach out to someone regularly unless it is a medical professional so that may be a challenge for experts to get frequent sessions.

Many potential users were not so interested in becoming an expert unless there was a need for information they felt confident in knowledge or a monetary benefit. That could present another challenge in finding experts for the app.

User Testing

Usability Test Plan

Before beginning the usability test, it was important for me to create a usability test plan, a document that outlines the scope, goals, and logistical details of my session in advance.

Usability Script

Usability Script With the Usability Test plan in mind, I created a usability test script. This is an outline of what I plan to say and communicate to my test participants. Scripts are incredibly useful and an important part of conducting an effective usability test.

Once I had all things in place, I conducted usability testing. I was able to test 6 participants who were all moderated remote tests with either their phone or computer. The tools I used were Google Meet and Screncast-o-matic.

Usability Test Results

I was able to collect the results from the usability tests and used affinity mapping to organize and categorize the participants quotes and errors.

Rainbow Spreadsheet

I then plugged the results into a rainbow spreadsheet which allowed me to itemize the errors and quotes from the usability test.

Usability Test Plan Report

The results from the usability test and the analysis then needs to be put into a report with the top 5 issues and changes. This is what would be shared with the stakeholders and developers.

Prototype

UI Style Guide

Now that the foundational design for ProConnect was complete, it was now time to refine the design.

I collected all of the design elements into a style guide. This would help clearly guide me in my design finalizations in making ProConnect a visually pleasing app as well as one the is user friendly and efficient.

Collaborative Design

With the UI detailed, I edited the wireframes to match the style guide details. I then shared my designs on a few forums to get feedback from other UX designers. I believe it is important to obtain feedback like this in order to keep true to the current design details and direction of UX.
